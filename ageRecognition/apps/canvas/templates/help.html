How to Play

In this section you have a short description of the game and its possibilities:

Profile

In this section you can keep track of your statistics. If you click the Upload button you will be able to add images to your gallery.

Game

This is the main section, four images are shown and you have to guess the age of all of them. The better your guess is the more points you get.

Achievements

Here you can see what you have achieved and what you need to do to get the next reward.

Gallery

In this section you will be able to see the images you have uploaded and see how many people have voted on them and get an estimation of your apparent age.

Ranking

The last section allows you to compare your score with your friends in the Friends Ranking and to all the players in the Global Ranking.
